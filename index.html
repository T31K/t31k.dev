Hello world! My name is Tim and I'm currently a tech consultant/developer. I hold all the repos below close to my heart as I was a self taught developer that "made it".

Initially I took it as a hobby & a way to past time during quarantine. Not long after, coding came very naturally for me & it ignited a spark within me, and with this burning passion, it pushed me to also realize that I wanted to pursue a career in this field. From then on, I started #100DaysofCode and promised myself I would learn anything it takes to get into the field and the rest was history...

I am proud to be a Self Taught Developer, throughout this time I have learned from many sources such as Harvard's edX, r/LearnProgramming, Udemy, Stack Overflow and freeCodeCamp. I love learning and believe it is a life-long journey to continually improve upon oneself.
