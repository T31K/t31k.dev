ABOUT ME

ex flight crew | present day dev

Hello, world! My name is Tim & I literally travel around the world, from breakfast to London and dinner at New York... or so I used to. Ever since the worldwide pandemic struck, I have been delligently learning coding from C to Python to JavaScript.

Initially I took it as a hobby & a way to past time during quarantine. Not long after, coding came very naturally for me & it ignited a flame within me, and with this burning passion, it pushed me to also realized that I wanted to pursue a career in this field. From then on, I started #100DaysofCode and promised myself I would learn anything it takes to get into the field and the rest was history...

I am proud to be a Self Taught Developer, throughout this time I have learned from many sources such as Harvard's edX, r/LearnProgramming, Udemy, Stack Overflow and freeCodeCamp. I love learning and believe it is a life-long journey to continually improve upon oneself.
